## Business Process Selection - Part 1

## 1. Viewing Events

### Business Process Name
User Viewing Behavior

### Business Questions
- What content is watched most often?
- What time of day do users watch the most?
- How long are users watching?
- Which genres perform best by region?
- Which subscription plans drive the most viewing hours?

### Grain of Fact Table
One row per **user viewing session for a content title on a device**.

### Source Systems Involved
- User database (PostgreSQL)
- Content catalog (MongoDB)
- Streaming events (Kafka)

---

## 2. Subscription Billing

### Business Process Name
Subscription Revenue and Churn

### Business Questions
- How much revenue is generated by plan and month?
- Which plans have the highest churn?
- How many new subscriptions are acquired each period?
- Which regions generate the most subscription revenue?
- What payment methods are most common?
- What are the total amount of users paying for each plan?

### Grain of Fact Table
One row per **billing transaction per user subscription event**.

### Source Systems Involved
- User database (PostgreSQL)
- Billing system (Stripe API)

---

## 3. Device Quality Monitoring

### Business Process Name
Streaming Device and Quality Analytics

### Business Questions
- Which devices generate the most playback failures?
- Which platforms have the highest buffering time?
- What streaming quality is most common by region?
- How does network type affect playback quality?
- Which device types are associated with session abandonment?
- Which device type is used most often?
- How many devices per account are used?

### Grain of Fact Table
One row per **playback quality event per viewing session and device**.

### Source Systems Involved
- Streaming events (Kafka)
- Device telemetry (IoT platform)
- User database (PostgreSQL)

---
